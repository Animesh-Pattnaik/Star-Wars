A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away....

Star wars depict the adventures of characters in which humans and many species of aliens co exist with droids who assist them in their daily routines. It contains many sci-fi objects such as space ships, asteroids, nebulae, space stations etc. The star wars franchise follows the epic fight between the good and evil, between the peacekeeper of the galaxy known as Jedi and the Sith who use the dark side of force by manipulating fear and aggression. The combination of science fiction and fantasy elements makes Star Wars a very universal franchise, capable of telling stories of various genres.
